About

Before I Was 'Amelia the Guide'

I was just a woman trying not to fall apart.

A mother. Afraid for her son's future. Watching the world crack open and wondering if there was any ground left to stand on.

I went to the mountains to escape — and realized you can't escape the truth. I stared at snow-covered peaks and couldn't smile. My heart was locked under a weight I couldn't name.

That was when I understood: I would never go back to the person I was before. Not because I lost something — but because something woke up.

The abilities I'd always had — seeing, feeling, knowing things I shouldn't know — stopped being a quirk and started being a calling.

I didn't choose this path. It chose me. And then it asked me to choose it back.

What I Actually Do

I am a conductor. A bridge between dimensions.

In a session with me, we don't sit and talk about your problems. We go — together — to the place where the problem started. A past life. An ancestral wound. A parallel reality where a contract was signed that you're still paying for.

We find it. We heal it. We rewrite it.

Not metaphorically. The women I work with report physical changes — chronic pain disappearing, eyesight improving mid-session, skin conditions clearing within days. They report life changes — new clients appearing, relationships transforming, careers shifting overnight.

I don't take credit for this. Your soul does the work. I just hold the space where it's safe enough to happen.

Why 'Awaken'

Because that's the only word that fits.

You're not learning something new. You're remembering something ancient. Something your soul has always known but your mind was trained to forget.

Every woman I work with already carries her power. My job is to help her stop apologizing for it.

Your awakening starts with one conversation.
